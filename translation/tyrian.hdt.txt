** Section 1 - Online Help - 255 chars max

~Start Level~  Select the next level to play (usually there is only one selection).
~开始关卡~  选择要玩的下个关卡（通常只有一个选择）。

~DATA~  Here you read the datacubes you collect during some levels.  Some cubes have certain... hints.  Be sure to read them!  Select DONE when you are finished reading your cubes.
~数据~  在这里您可以阅读在某些关卡中收集的数据方块。有些方块包含某些……提示。一定要阅读它们！阅读完您的方块后点“完成”。

~Player Input~  Select the input device you want to use by pushing left or right.  Tyrian will not allow both players to use the same input device.
~玩家输入~  通过按左右键选择您想要使用的输入设备。泰瑞安不允许两名玩家使用相同的输入设备。

-nogood-

~Upgrade Ship~  You can configure many aspects of your ship.  Upgrade points for your ship are gained by shooting enemies and collecting various items.
~升级飞船~  您可以配置飞船的许多方面。通过射击敌人和收集各种物品来获得飞船的升级点数。

~Purchasing~  Use the up/down arrow keys to select the item you want to view.  When you purchase the item, a bar will move to show the item you have purchased.  You may then exit the menu using done or ESC.
~购买~  使用上下方向键选择您想要查看的物品。购买物品时，会出现一个指示条来显示您已购买的物品。然后您可以点“完成”或ESC键退出菜单。

The amount of money you will have available after purchasing the item selected is shown at the bottom.  Items that are darkened out cost more than you can afford and you will be unable to select them.
在购买所选物品后您将拥有的可用资金的数额显示在底部。价格超出您承受能力的物品会变暗，您将无法选择它们。

~Ship Type~  Each ship has a different armor rating.  Armor is your last line of protection from enemy attack and is not easily repaired in the middile of combat.
~飞船类型~  每艘飞船都有不同的装甲等级。装甲是您抵御敌人攻击的最后一道防线，在战斗中不容易修复。

~Front Gun~/~Rear Gun~  Your ship can carry both a front and rear gun.  You can change your weapons or upgrade them from here.
~前炮~/~后炮~  您的飞船可以携带一对前炮和后炮。您可以在这里更换或升级武器。

To ~upgrade~ or ~downgrade~ your existing gun, use the right and left arrow keys or click on the red arrows below your ship.  The bars in the center show you what power level your weapons are at.
要~升级~或~降级~现有武器，请使用左右方向键或点击飞船下方的红色箭头。中间的进度条显示您的武器处于何种威力等级。

The red arrows and cost displays indicate when you can upgrade or downgrade your weapons.  If the right arrow is darkened or the cost is displayed in red, you cannot upgrade further.
红色箭头和费用显示会提示您何时可以升级或降级武器。如果右箭头变暗或费用显示为红色，则表示无法进一步升级。

-nogood-

Be sure to watch the power bar to see how quickly your energy level will drop while firing.
请留意能量条，以了解开火时能量下降的速度。

~Shield~  Your ship is protected by a shield, which you upgrade in the same manner as your ship. Just select the shield you like with the up/down arrow keys, and buy it by pressing the "enter" key or the space bar.
~护盾~  您的飞船有护盾保护，您可以通过与升级飞船相同的方式升级护盾。只需使用上下方向键选择您喜欢的护盾， 然后按回车键或空格键购买。

~Generator~  You need power to fly that ship you're in. Your generator is the most important part of your ship. It determines how many shots you can fire at a time as well as how fast your shield recharges.
~发电机~  您需要电力来驾驶飞船。您的发电机是飞船最重要的部分。它决定了您一次能发射多少炮弹以及护盾的恢复速度。

Buy a better generator and you'll have a much better edge. Select your option in the same manner as the other items.
购买更好的发电机，您将获得更加强大的优势。选择方式与其它物品相同。

~Sidekicks~  You can have both a left and right sidekick.  These are special weapons that fly alongside your ship.
~副武器~  您可以拥有一对左右副武器。这些是能与您的飞船并肩飞行的特殊武器。

They range from just a simple cannon to the awesome "Atom" bomb. Some are much more powerful than others.  Many have limited ammo, but they will recharge slowly during a level!
它们从简单的加农炮到威力惊人的“原子”炸弹应有尽有。有些武器的威力远超其他武器。许多武器的弹药有限，但会在关卡中会慢慢补充！

-nogood-

~Done~  This selection allows you to return to the Main Menu. Click on it when you're ready to fly!
~完成~  选择此项可让您返回主菜单。准备好飞行时点击它！

~Options Menu~  From this menu you may configure your joystick or keyboard, and load and save your games.
~选项菜单~  从这个菜单中您可以配置操纵杆或键盘，以及加载和保存游戏。

~Save Game~  The game you have in progress is automatically saved when you finish the level. So if you forget to save your game, just load "Last Level" and the last level you played will be loaded.
~保存游戏~  您正在进行的游戏会在完成关卡时自动保存。所以如果您忘记保存游戏，只需加载“上一关卡”，您上次玩的关卡就会被加载。

To save a game select "save game" from the Options menu, find an empty slot, press the Enter key or spacebar, and enter the name of your choice.
要保存游戏，请从选项菜单中选择“保存游戏”，找到一个空槽，按回车键或空格键，然后输入您选择的名称。

~Load Game~  To load a game, follow the same procedure except all you need to do is highlight the name of the game you wish to load and press Enter or hit the spacebar.
~加载游戏~  要加载游戏，请按照相同的步骤操作，只需高亮您想要加载的游戏名称，然后按回车键或空格键即可。

~Keyboard~  To configure your keyboard select "Keyboard" from the Options menu. Then, hit the key you wish to use for the function that is highlighted (example: when "up" is highlighted, hit the up arrow on your numeric keypad).
~键盘~  要配置键盘，请从选项菜单中选择“键盘”。然后，按高亮显示的功能所对应的键（例如：当“上”被高亮时按数字小键盘的上方向键）。

~Joystick~  To configure your joystick, simply select "Joystick" from the Options menu and follow the on-screen instructions.
~操纵杆~  要配置您的操纵杆，只需从选项菜单中选择“操纵杆”，然后按照屏幕上的说明操作即可。

~Volume~  You may also change the volume of the music or the sound in the game by highlighting either "Music" or "Sound" in the Options menu and using the left and right arrow keys to change the volume.
~音量~  您还可以通过在选项菜单中高亮显示“音乐”或“音效”，然后按左右方向键来更改游戏中的音乐或音效音量。

~Quit Game~  Select this to quit your game.  You will be returned to the title screen.  Use Alt-X for a quick way to quit the game.
~退出游戏~  选择此选项可退出游戏。您将返回到标题画面。使用Alt-X可快速退出游戏。

To exit the game from the title screen simply select "Quit Game" again and press Enter or the spacebar.
要从标题画面退出游戏，请再次选择“退出游戏”，然后按回车键或空格键。

Select Ordering Info from Main Menu to find out how to order.
从主菜单选择“订购信息”以了解如何订购。

~Power Bar~  Current power available.  Your weapons and shields draw power from here.
~能量条~  当前可用能量。您的武器和护盾从这里摄取能量。

~Weapons~  The two bars show your weapon power for both front and rear weapons.
~武器~  两个进度条分别显示前后武器的威力。

~Weapon Mode~  Some rear weapons can have their firing pattern changed with the keyboard command ~change fire~ or a joystick button assigned to ~Change rear weapon~.
~武器模式~  某些后武器可通过~改变射击模式~键盘命令或~改变后武器~操纵杆按钮来改变射击方式。

~Sidekicks~  You can have up to two side weapons, usually fired with separate buttons.  The number of shots left are shown under the weapon name.
~副武器~  您最多可以拥有两个副武器，通常用单独的按钮发射。剩余的射击次数显示在武器名下方。

~Shield/Armor~  These bars show how much damage you can take.  Shields will recharge with time from your ~power bar~ and a repair ship may appear if your armor is low - shoot it!
~护盾/装甲~  这俩条显示能承受的伤害量。护盾会利用~能量条~随时间自动恢复，若装甲过低，可能出现维修船，射击它！

~Side-Panel~  Top: Player 1    Bottom: Player 2
~侧面板~  上：玩家1    下：玩家2

~Weapon Power~  This shows the level of power for each player's main weapon.
~武器威力~  显示每个玩家主武器的威力等级。

~Linking~  Move both ships together to combine them.  Player 1 disengages by holding down a sidekick firing button and moving up.  Player 2 disengages by moving without firing the main gun.
~链接~  移动两艘飞船使其靠拢来组合。玩家1通过按住副武器射击键并向上移动来脱离。玩家2通过不发射主炮而移动来脱离。

~Rotation Cannon~  Player 2 can aim the cannon by holding down the fire button and moving in any direction.
~旋转炮~  玩家2可以通过按住射击按钮，并朝任意方向移动来移动炮的瞄准方向。

~USP鹰爪号~为您提供了~整整一英寸厚~的装甲板，以及宽敞的全尺寸发电机和引擎区域。它配备了前后安装系统和两个货舱，就像真正的~微索~战机一样！

The craft is especially designed for speed and maneuverability; it gives you an unparalleled 3G base thrust addition!  All this for only ~6000~ cr!!
这款飞船专为速度和机动性而设计，为您提供无与伦比的3G基础推力加成！仅售~6000~cr！

The ~SuperCarrot FoodShip 9~ is one of the most advanced edible fighters ever produced.  You can't go wrong with a ~FoodShip~!
~超级胡萝卜食品飞船9号~是迄今为止生产的最先进的可食用战机。选择~食品飞船~准没错！

~Foodships~ are usually complemented by either the ~Hotdog Mustard-spread Technology~, or the dreaded ~SuperBanana Bombs~.
~食品飞船~通常配备~热狗芥末涂抹技术~或令人畏惧的~超级香蕉炸弹~。

The ~Gencore Phoenix~ is the main advanced ~Gencore~ ~Solo~ ~Fighter~.  It's an extremely reliable craft that can take a good deal of punishment.
~基核凤凰号~是~基核单人战机~系列中的主力先进机型。它是一款极其可靠的飞船，能够承受相当大的打击。

Standard on the ~Gencore~ ~Solo~ ~Fighter~ line is the new particle redefinition technology, which allows you to materialize extra armor on your outer hull.  Pilots are warned that the system is easily triggered and it completely drains your shields.
~基核单人战机~系列的标准配置是新的粒子重定义技术，该技术允许您在外壳上生成额外的装甲。飞行员请注意，该系统容易触发，并且会完全耗尽您的护盾。

The ~Gencore Maelstrom~ is a refined ~Phoenix~ with an additional ~2cm~ of main hull.  It also features deluxe plush seating with ~extra-wide~ ~armrests~ and ~synth-velour~ ~lining~.
~基核旋涡号~是~凤凰号~的改进版，主船体增加了~2厘米~。它还配备了豪华的毛绒座椅，配有~超宽扶手~和~合成天鹅绒衬里~。

~Microsol~.  What more do you need to convince you that their ~Stalker Solo Fighter~ is the ship you want?  Not so fast, though.  You'll pay a hefty premium for this ship or have to fill out a stack of forms two feet high.
~微索~。还有什么能比这更让您确信他们的~追踪者单人战机~就是您想要的飞船呢？不过，且慢。您得为这艘飞船支付高昂的溢价，或者填写两英尺高的表格。

With its dense hull alloys and overall excellent defensive capability, you'll sleep safer at night with one of these on your side.
凭借其致密的船体合金和出色的防御能力，有这样一艘飞船在身边，您晚上会睡得更安稳。

~Microsol~ continues the ~Stalker~ line with the ~Stalker-B~.  This is the deluxe model preferred by Microsol's own Outer Scouts.  It's the ship of choice for those long, solo flights.
~微索~继续推出了~追踪者~系列的~追踪者-B~型号。这是微索自身外太空侦察队偏爱的豪华型号。对于那些漫长的单人飞行来说，这是首选飞船。

The ~Stalker-B~ adds an additional ~2~ ~structural~ ~units~ with its refined alloys, for an unheard-of ~28 SUs~ in a solo craft.
~追踪者-B~型号采用精炼合金，额外增加了~2个结构单元~，使得单人飞船的结构单位达到了前所未有的~28个结构单元~。

~TOP SECRET:~ The quest continues for the strongest ship available with the most powerful backup defense systems.  This is one of the only ships in existence with 3 defense bays!
~绝密:~ 寻找最强飞船以及最强大的备用防御系统的征程仍在继续。这是现存为数不多拥有3个防御舱的飞船之一！

An additional SU has been added to the armor value of this ship, and Microsol has retrofitted a ~Gencore~ ~auto-repair~ ~system~ into each ship, making these the definite superiority fighter!
这艘飞船的护甲值增加了一个额外的结构单元，而且微索还为每艘飞船都加装了~基核自动修复系统~，使其成为绝对的制胜战机！

The ~USP Fang~ is a standard ~Talon~ that has been retrofitted with additional armor and a more powerful secondary backup defense system.
~USP舫~是一款标准的~鹰爪~飞船，加装了额外的护甲和更强大的备用防御系统。

As always, the ~USP~ invulnerability system is unique in its field and, if used properly, will let you take out anything else in the universe.
